Scope and Strategy

This website was made to showcase my work for this module during the first semester and gather all four tasks (CARP, Typography, UX and Coding Worksheets) in one place. It should be user friendly and accessible through all devices. A basic but elegant design would serve the purpose of the website, which is to show the posts and other visual work made without stealing the light from them. My audience is the lecturers and leaders of GWDS module and others from Digital Media course, classmates, and probably family and friends. It could also be used in the future on my portfolio to show clients and employers.

The website will mainly consist of images and text content from the four tasks, with some GIFs/videos for decoration purposes. It will have a page for each task, in addition to the home page and “about me” page. They will be linked through a navigation menu on the top of the website. The content will be displayed as one section on each page. Simple, straightforward, and minimal.
