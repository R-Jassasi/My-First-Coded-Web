Thoughts

This task was the third most enjoyable one after styling my website and the typo task. It was relatively easy for I have some work experience as a graphic designer. However, although I had many ideas, time wasn’t by my side, so I couldn’t try them all. As you can see, I made one all from scratch using shapes and vectors (The one displayed on top) which consumed the longest amount of time among them all. I made the other two using a photo and blew a faint retro spirit on them.

It was fun and I would love to do more tasks of this kind:)
